Welcome and thank you for participating in our study on quality of Web data.

The study considers the following scenario:
- Suppose you are asked to write an article about debate on vaccinations triggered by the measles outbreak in 2015 at Disneyland.
- For this, you are asked to identify a set of reliable Web documents to serve as sources of information for your article.
- We have selected 50 documents from the Web relevant to this topic.
- In this experiment, we will present to you a number of pages with a random selection of 6 documents (from the set of 50).
- On each page you will see different criteria to help you make your choice, e.g. keywords contained in the document, the sentiment in the document, etc.
- Your goal is to select the documents you think are most reliable and motivate your choices.

At the end we will ask you to reflect on your experiences during this task, and share with us how it aligns with your typical research process.
